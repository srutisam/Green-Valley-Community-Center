Sunrise Yoga

Participants gathered early in the morning to experience a serene yoga session led by our expert instructors. The event took place in our beautiful outdoor garden, providing a tranquil setting with the sounds of nature as a backdrop.

Green Screen

The event featured a classic film screening, complete with popcorn and refreshments. It was a wonderful opportunity for older adults to socialize, reminisce, and enjoy a nostalgic cinematic experience in a comfortable setting.

Garden To Table

Participants learned how to harvest fresh ingredients from the community garden and transform them into delicious meals. The class emphasized sustainable practices and healthy eating, while enjoying a communal meal, surrounded by the beauty of nature.


Green Valley Music

The Green Valley Music rocked the community with performances from local bands and musicians in an outdoor venue. The lively atmosphere, set against the backdrop of Green Valley's natural beauty, made it a highlight of the summer season.

Family Fun Day

Families enjoyed a nature scavenger hunt, tree planting, and a guided nature walk. The event culminated in an outdoor picnic, bringing the community together to celebrate the beauty of Green Valley's natural surroundings.

Wildlife Watch

Families and nature enthusiasts joined local naturalists for guided walks to observe and identify birds, mammals, and insects in their natural habitats. The event included educational talks on wildlife conservation and the importance of biodiversity.


Nature Photography Workshop

Led by a professional photographer, participants learned techniques for capturing the beauty of the local flora and fauna. The workshop included a collage of all the captures pictures in the end.

Community Tree Planting

Volunteers of all ages came together to plant and nurture native trees in designated neighbourhoods and areas around the community. The event aimed to enhance local green spaces , promote environmental consciousness and stewardship while adding to surrounding aesthetic.

Composting 101 Workshop

Attendees learned the basics of composting, including how to start a compost bin, and the benefits of composting for soil health and the environment. The workshop included hands-on demonstrations and take-home resources. Attendees received step-by-step instructions.


Holiday Green Craft Bazaar

Local artisans and crafters showcased their eco-friendly, handmade goods, from jewelry and pottery to holiday decorations and gifts. Attendees enjoyed the goodies, all while supporting local and environmentally conscious businesses.
